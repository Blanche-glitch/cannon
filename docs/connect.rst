How to Connect My Canon Printer to My Computer?
-------------

**Step 1 - Turn on your Canon printer**

- Press the power button and ensure the printer is properly powered on.

**Step 2 - Choose connection method**

- Decide whether you want to connect using a USB cable or WiFi.

**Step 3 - Connect using USB (Wired Method)**

- Plug the USB cable into your printer and computer to establish a direct connection.

**Step 4 - Wait for device detection**

- Allow your computer to automatically detect and install the printer drivers.

**Step 5 - Follow on-screen instructions**

- Complete any setup prompts that appear on your screen.

**Step 6 - Connect using WiFi (Wireless Method)**

- Open printer settings and select Wireless LAN Setup to begin connection.

**Step 7 - Select your WiFi network**

- Choose your network name from the list of available connections.

**Step 8 - Enter WiFi password**

- Type the correct password to connect the printer to your network.

**Step 9 - Add printer to your computer**

- Go to printer settings and select “Add a Printer” to complete setup.

**Step 10 - Print a test page**

- Print a sample document to confirm your printer is connected successfully.

Connection completed successfully.
