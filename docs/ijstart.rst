IJ Start Canon Printer Setup
-------------

**Step 1 - Open your web browser**

- Go to your web browser and open the official Canon setup page to begin.

**Step 2 - Enter your printer model**

- Type your Canon printer model number to find the correct setup file.

**Step 3 - Download the setup software**

- Click the download button and save the installer on your computer.

**Step 4 - Run the setup file**

- Open the downloaded file and click Run to start the installation process.

**Step 5 - Accept terms and conditions**

- Follow the on-screen instructions and accept the license agreement.

**Step 6 - Choose connection method**

- Select WiFi or USB connection based on your preference.

**Step 7 - Connect the printer**

- Follow instructions to connect your printer to your computer or WiFi network.

**Step 8 - Complete installation**

- Continue the setup until the installation finishes successfully.

**Step 9 - Add printer to your device**

- Go to printer settings and ensure your Canon printer is listed.

**Step 10 - Print a test page**

- Print a sample document to confirm the setup is complete and working properly.

Setup completed successfully.
