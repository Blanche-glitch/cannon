Install My Canon Printer to Computer
====================================

Follow these steps:

Step 1 - Open your web browser and go to the Canon setup page to begin the installation process.

Step 2 - Enter your printer model number and download the correct setup file for your system.

Step 3 - Locate the downloaded file on your computer and double-click it to start the installer.

Step 4 - Click Run when prompted and allow the installation wizard to launch.

Step 5 - Follow the on-screen instructions and accept the license agreement to proceed.

Step 6 - Choose your preferred connection method such as USB or WiFi during setup.

Step 7 - Continue with the guided instructions until the installation is completed.

Step 8 - Restart your computer if required to finalize the setup.

Step 9 - Open printer settings on your computer and ensure your Canon printer is listed.

Step 10 - Print a test page to confirm the printer is installed and working properly.

Your printer is ready to use.
