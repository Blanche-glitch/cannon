Install My Canon Printer to Computer
-------------

**Step 1 - Open your web browser**

- Go to your web browser and open the Canon setup page to begin installation.

**Step 2 - Enter your printer model**
- Type your Canon printer model number to find the correct setup file.

**Step 3 - Download the setup file**
- Click the download button and save the installer on your computer.

**Step 4 - Run the installer**
- Open the downloaded file and click Run to start the installation process.

**Step 5 - Follow on-screen instructions**
- Proceed with the setup wizard and accept the license agreement.

**Step 6 - Select connection method**
- Choose USB or WiFi based on your preferred setup option.

**Step 7 - Complete the installation**
- Continue following instructions until the setup is fully completed.

**Step 8 - Restart your computer**
- Restart your system if prompted to finalize the installation.

**Step 9 - Check printer settings**
- Open printer settings and confirm your Canon printer is listed.

**Step 10 - Print a test page**
- Print a sample page to ensure your printer is working correctly.
