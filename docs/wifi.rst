How to Connect Canon Printer to WiFi
-------------

**Step 1 - Turn on your Canon printer**

- Press the power button and make sure your printer is ready for setup.

**Step 2 - Open printer settings**

- Use the printer control panel to access the setup or network settings menu.

**Step 3 - Select Wireless LAN Setup**

- Choose the wireless setup option to begin connecting to a network.

**Step 4 - Choose your WiFi network**

- Select your network name (SSID) from the available list.

**Step 5 - Enter your WiFi password**

- Input the correct password to connect your printer to the network.

**Step 6 - Confirm the connection**

- Wait for the printer to display a confirmation message.

**Step 7 - Connect your computer to the same network**

- Ensure your computer is using the same WiFi network as the printer.

**Step 8 - Add the printer to your system**

- Open printer settings on your computer and click “Add a Printer.”

**Step 9 - Complete setup**

- Follow the on-screen instructions to finish adding the printer.

**Step 10 - Print a test page**

- Print a document to verify your Canon printer is connected to WiFi successfully.
